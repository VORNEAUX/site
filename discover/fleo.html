THE PROBLEM

Four questions every freelancer avoids — until they can't.

None of these are complicated to answer. They're just tedious enough that most people never sit down and do the math — and expensive enough when they get it wrong.

01

"Am I actually charging enough?"

Most freelancers pick a rate by guessing, copying a competitor, or anchoring to their old salary — not by working backward from what they need to take home after tax.

02

"How much should I be setting aside for taxes?"

Get it wrong and you either overpay all year or face a bill you can't cover in April. Flat "set aside 30%" rules ignore your actual country, bracket, and deductions.

03

"Is this client actually worth my time?"

Revenue looks healthy on paper. Net profit — after your own hours are costed in — sometimes tells a very different story, and most freelancers never calculate it.

04

"Why does invoicing eat an entire afternoon?"

A new document, re-typing client details, second-guessing the format — for something that should take sixty seconds once your details are on file.

FAQ

Questions worth answering before you buy.

Is my data really safe? Where does it go?+
Everything you enter is saved automatically to your browser's local storage on your own device. FLEO™ has no servers and nothing is ever uploaded — your financial data never leaves your computer or phone unless you export a backup yourself.
Is this a subscription?+
No. FLEO™ is a one-time purchase through Gumroad. You download the file once and it's yours to use for as long as you want — no recurring charge, ever.
Does it work on Mac, PC, and mobile?+
Yes. FLEO™ runs in any modern browser on Windows, macOS, iOS, and Android. The one difference is PDF generation — on mobile, invoices download as an HTML file you open and print or share from your browser, since mobile handles print dialogs differently than desktop.
Are the tax numbers accurate enough to file with?+
Tax Vault uses real progressive brackets and major social contributions for each supported country, and is built for planning and budgeting — knowing roughly what to set aside and when. Personal circumstances (dependents, additional income, local surcharges) can shift the final number, so always confirm with a tax professional before filing.
How do I move my data to a new device?+
Go to Settings → Export Backup on your current device — this downloads a JSON file. Transfer it to your other device however you like (email, cloud drive, AirDrop), then open FLEO™ there and use Settings → Import Backup. Everything restores instantly, since there's no automatic cloud sync by design.
What if I lose the file or my computer breaks?+
As long as you've exported a backup at some point (one click, five seconds), you can restore everything on a new device via Import Backup. The guide walks you through making this a monthly habit from day one.
Do you offer refunds?+
Purchases are handled through Gumroad's standard checkout and refund policy. If FLEO™ isn't right for you, reach out and we'll take care of it.
What exactly do I get after purchase?+
A download containing the FLEO™ system file and the full Getting Started Guide — a companion walkthrough covering every tool, a 6-step first session, best practices, and troubleshooting. No account or installer needed.
